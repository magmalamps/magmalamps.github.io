How to build your own lava lamp


What materials do I need?

-A bottle made of glass
-Oil
-2 or 3 Effervescent tablets

How to

Fill the bottle with water to around 50%
Add some Oil until it is filled up to 1/3
Place the bottle on top of a LED or lightsource
Put in the Effervescent tablets and watch the magic
